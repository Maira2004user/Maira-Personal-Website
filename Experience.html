What I do for my 2 months of internship at An-Nur Speacialist Hospital?

During my two months internship, I was assigned to the Information Technology (IT) Department, which consist of the application and technical teams. I had the opportunity to work with both teams, I gained diverse experiences. Early on, I participated in a User Acceptance Test (UAT) project, helping staff evaluate a potential new system by identfying issues, bug and weaknesses. My role involved assisting users in exploring and reviewing the system. I also attended a Hospital Information System (HIS) meeting, where IT staff analyzed a vendor's system presentation to assess its suitability for the hospital.

Additionally, I supported the technical team with tasks such as replacing PCs, troubleshooting laptops and AIO computers, fixing printers, checking Anydesk connections, IPTV setups, and updating the Points Of Care (POC) inventory list. Besides, I also assisted in setting up HIS, transferring data, fixing clinic telephones, and learning about servers, networks, and security. Overall, my responsibilities included resolving technical issues, assisting with system setup, reviewing operations, performing data patches, creating pivot tables, and updating inventory reports in Excel.
